Pen and Ink

Challenge Statement

Use micron pens and water color to present a detailed drawing of assorted items. The drawing must show detail and use cross-hatchings hatching, scribbling and stippling.

Solution Statement

I first arranged the items in person, and then I took photos to refer back to when I'm drawing. I first sketched out the outlines of the drawings, and then I added water color on top of the drawing. I then added values to show the details.
