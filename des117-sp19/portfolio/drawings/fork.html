Household Item

Challenge Statement

Choose a common household object and make a drawing of the object in extreme close-up. Choose a view of the object that is unusual, so that it might not be recognizable at first glance. Use at least five different values in the drawing and use lots of detail.

Solution Statement

I first took a photo of the fork in the lighting, so I could refer back to it and keep the light direction consistent. I made multiple sketches of the photo before I started on the large sized paper because I wanted to plan out the values I wanted to use. I used hatching, cross-hatching, scribbling, and stippling.
