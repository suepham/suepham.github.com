Household Item

This project looked at common household objects with a lot of texture and to draw the object up close. I chose to do a oyster fork in the reflecting light because the fork is simple yet a bit different from the normal forks.

Pen and Ink

In this project, I had to incorporate multiple items (feather, sphere, twig, rock, blanket, and woven basket) using micron pens and water color. I took pictures of the objects first and then drew it.
