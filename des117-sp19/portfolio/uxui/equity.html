Equity Project

Challenge Statement

Design, build and user test a prototype for a web or mobile application that solves a specific problem for a target audience.

Solution Statement

I used Sketch to create the layout of this app, so I can layout the designs of the app screens. I chose to design an app that allows designers to post their work on the app and get feedback from other designers. I thought that since design classrooms often do not have enough time for the instructor to spend enough time with each student to provide quality feedback, getting feedback from other people will help point out issues or how to improve. The user can add photos, videos, links, texts, or gifs on the app and get feedback from other designers. The posts can be edited or deleted, and the feedback comments can also be edited and deleted. Users can view their profile and search in the app for posts from other users. I specifically wanted this to be a mobile app only because the photos are easily accessible and many people have a phone.
