Front Page for a Non-Profit Organization

Challenge Statement

Design a front page of a website for a non-profit organization that looks professional and would appeal to young adults diagnosed with cancer and their families. I wanted to encourage users within that target audience to come to a cycling event and to donate to the organization.

Solution Statement

I used Sketch to create the layout of this website because I wanted to learn and use the software. I chose to use the colors grey, white, and red for a professional look in the website, and the color red was used to stand out to tie the content together. I added the evenet at the beginning as one of the first call-to-action, and the donation section is placed later on after the user reads some information about the organization and the comunity. I added a section to read stories because I wanted the audience to understand that there are others like them and that they are not alone. They can come to this site to find stories and resources.
