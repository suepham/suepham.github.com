Of All Things

Challenge Statement

Look at two contrasting words and create an interesting visual of the relationship in the words.

Solution Statement

I used a storyboard to plan out the sequence before I did it in Adobe AfterEffects. I chose to do the words "absorb" and "repel" because the words are action words, so it would have a realistic interpretation of the words in the motion. I used a couple shapes in the sequence because I wanted to focus on the meaning in the words rather than focusing on other visuals. I showed the relationship with the two words by having the word "repel" get absorbed by the word "absorb," and I had the word "absorb" get repelled whenever it was near "repel." In the end, I had both repel and absorb work together to defeat the foreign shape, and in the end they are balanced with each other.
