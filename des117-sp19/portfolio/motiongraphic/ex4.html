Web in Motion

Challenge Statement

Create a logo that transitions into a website, and show the motion of moving through the webpage.

Solution Statement

I used a storyboard to plan out the sequence before I did it in Adobe AfterEffects. I used the lines from the hashtag to show the rest of the logo because I wanted the entire logo to interact with each other. I added a loading circle at the bottom, so that it could show that there's more to the sequence. I had each part of the blog load in different directions, so it is similar to how real webpages load. I hovered over the search icon and posts so users can see when there was a clickable link. I scrolled through the page to show the rest of the content.
