Sustainability - Energy

Challenge Statement

Create a motion sequence using graphics and typography to bring awareness to a topic in sustainability.

Solution Statement

I chose energy as my topic because there are simple changes that people in the world can make to save energy and protect the environment. I found images on Google to create a typography and grpahics moodboard. I created two styleframes to see two stylistic approaches I could take in the video. I used a storyboard to plan out the sequence before I did it in Adobe AfterEffects. I tried to balance out the graphics and typography by having sequences where the graphic would support the information in the type, some sequences where the type would support the graphic. I wanted the audience to become aware of the problem, and I then listed three things they could do to help the problem.
