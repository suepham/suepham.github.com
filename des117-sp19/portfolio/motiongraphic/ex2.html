Times of Your Life

Challenge Statement

Film a moving object in real life, and recreate the motion and visuals with typography.

Solution Statement

I used a storyboard to plan out the sequence before I did it in Adobe AfterEffects. I decided to trace the motion of a coffee machine pouring coffee into a mug. I created the mug with text, and I used a gradient in the to create depth in the mug. The main motion of the sequence was that the coffee was falling into the mug.
