Massimo Vignelli

Challenge Statement

Create a motion sequence that balances both graphics and typography, and explains the legacy of a deceased artist or designer.

Solution Statement

I chose to do Massimo Vignelli because I liked his work with the New York station map and his logos for companies. I looked at images on Google to create a moodboard for the graphics and type. I also created two styleframes, so I could see the theme of the video. I used a storyboard to plan out the sequence before I did it in Adobe AfterEffects. I then created the motions by mimicking some of the images I found on Google, and I used a quote from Massimo Vignelli to show throughout the sequence.
