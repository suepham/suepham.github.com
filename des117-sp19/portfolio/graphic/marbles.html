Vexel Trace of Marbles

Challenge Statement

Retrace a stock image to improve the quality of the image, so a clearer image can be used.

Solution Statement

I traced a stock image of marbles because the image was very pixelated when it was zoomed in. I used Adobe Illustrator to recreate the image because vectors are shapes and lines that will not get pixelated. I traced the lines of the image underneath, so I can get the most precise copy of the marbles, and I also used the eyedropper tool to match the colors exactly.
