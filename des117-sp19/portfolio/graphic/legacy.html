Legacy Poster

Challenge Statement

Create a poster using Adobe Photoshop that features the legacy of a leader in arts or science. The poster should show the person’s life and work, and it should look like it might be used in an exhibit.

Solution Statement

I placed his name and the years he was alive at the bottom because I thought his legacy work was more significant than the text. I had an image of Charles Dickens at the bottom, so it appeared like he was reflecting on his work in his lifetime. I had illustrations next to the books that he wrote, so the audience could see what his books were about. I used Baskerville as the typeface because it’s mainly used in books.
