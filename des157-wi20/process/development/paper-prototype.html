Paper-Prototype

These are the paper prototypes for three sections on my website, and they have the most interactive elements. The prototypes show the sequence of how the interactivity plays out from one part to the next.

In the manufacturing section, the user holds down the spacebar to fill the oil up.

In the packaging section, the user selects all the items in the list.

In the transportation section, the user once again holds down the spacebar to move the semi-truck.
