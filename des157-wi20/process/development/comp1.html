Comp V1

This is the first version of my final project website. It brings the user into the life of a plastic bottle by following its entire lifecycle. Once the user selects "Start the Experience," they will press the spacebar to see how much oil is put into making one bottle. Then they will select the checklist items to see what is required to package the plastic bottles. The user will then press the spacebar again to transport, and they will click the "BUY" button. At the end, the user will make a final decision on how the plastic bottle will end up.
